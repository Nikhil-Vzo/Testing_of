PRODUCT OVERVIEW: OthrHalff
===========================

WHAT IS OthrHalff?
------------------
OthrHalff is a specialized dating and social discovery platform designed exclusively for university campuses. It helps students find their "Other Half" - whether that's a romantic partner, a gym buddy, a study partner, or just a new friend.

In a world of generic dating apps, OthrHalff brings verification and relevance, ensuring you only connect with real students from your campus or nearby universities.

TARGET AUDIENCE
---------------
- University Students
- Anyone looking for meaningful connections on campus (Love, Friendship, Activities)

KEY FEATURES
------------
1. DISCOVER FEED
   - Swipe Right to Like, Left to Pass.
   - Infinite recycled feed ensures you never run out of profiles (gives you a second chance at missed connections).
   - Smart algorithm mixes fresh faces with people you might have missed.

2. MATCHES & CHAT
   - Real-time messaging with instant notifications.
   - Audio & Video Calling: Built-in reliable calling features with custom ringtones.
   - Unread Badges: Always know when someone is trying to reach you.

3. CONFESSIONS
   - An anonymous (or public) space to share thoughts, campus gossip, or secrets.
   - Interactive polls to gauge campus opinion.

4. VIRTUAL DATES
   - Watch content together in real-time (Cinema Mode).
   - Bridging the gap between matching and meeting in person.

5. VERIFIED PROFILES
   - University email verification required.
   - Detailed profiles with interests, avatars, and bio.

BRAND IDENTITY & DESIGN SYSTEM

COLOR PALETTE
The app features a "Neon & Dark" aesthetic, designed to feel premium, modern, and exciting (Night/Club vibes).

- Primary Neon Pink: #ff007f  (Used for accents, buttons, glows, and the logo)
- Background Dark:   #050505  (Deep Black)
- Card Background:   #121212  (Off-Black)
- Text Primary:      #ffffff  (White)

LOGO CONSTRUCTION (Code Reference)
The logo is built using code (React/Tailwind) rather than an image file, ensuring perfect scalability.

Component Logic:
1. Icon: A 'Ghost' icon (Lucide React) rotated slightly on hover.
   - Color: #ff007f
   - Effect: Drop Shadow (0 0 8px rgba(255,0,127,0.5))
   - Decoration: A small 'Sparkle' icon animating on the top right.

2. Typography:
   - Font: Sans-serif, Extra Bold / Black weight.
   - Text "Othr": White
   - Text "Halff": Neon Pink (#ff007f)
   - Layout: Flex row, tight tracking (tracking-tighter), uppercase.
